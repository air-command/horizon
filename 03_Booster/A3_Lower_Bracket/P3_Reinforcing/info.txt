After the bracket is glued to the bracket pads with 24hour Araldite, each joint is reinforced from the outside with 10 layers of 85gsm fiberglass cloth. Each layer alternates in orientation 0-90 and 45-45.

The inside corner is reinforced with 6 alternating 0-90 and 45-45 layers of 85gsm cloth all the same size 60x35mm
